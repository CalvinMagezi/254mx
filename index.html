Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does it take to be a rider?

    Skill, discipline, dedication, consistency. Upon these three pillars you can build the foundation you will need to overcome your everyday challenges, and find balance in your everyday life. Most importantly, make sure you're enjoying yourself. If it's not fun it's not worth it.

  • What does it take to be a champion?

    As core values, you require the 3 P's; Patience Passion & Perserverance. You are going to need the patience to undergo intense and repetitive deliberate practice programs. You are going to need the passion to push yourself out of your comfort zone time and time again. Last but not least you will need the perserverance to keep getting back up after every fall.

  • At what age can I start riding?

    I personally started riding at age 7. However, today that's considered a little bit late. There are children riding in the pee wee class from ages as young as 3 years old.
